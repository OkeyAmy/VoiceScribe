VoiceScribe Help

Getting Started

VoiceScribe lets you record, transcribe, and process your voice in multiple languages including English, Igbo, Yoruba, Hausa, and Nigerian Pidgin.

Features

  • Record: Click the microphone button to start/stop recording.
  • Polished Notes: Automatically removes filler words and formats your speech.
  • Summaries: Generate concise summaries of your recordings.
  • Translations: Translate your notes into different languages.

Language Support

VoiceScribe can transcribe mixed languages in the same recording. Speak naturally, even when switching between languages.

Local Dialect Features

This app is specially designed to understand Nigerian languages and dialects, including:

  • Igbo: Recognizes tonal patterns and dialectal variations
  • Yoruba: Handles diacritical marks and tonal inflections
  • Hausa: Understands contextual meanings and idiomatic expressions
  • Nigerian Pidgin: Processes code-switching and regional expressions
